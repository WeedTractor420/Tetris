How to Play Tetris

Controls

  • Move Left: Press A
  • Move Right: Press D
  • Move Down: Press S
  • Rotate Right: Press W
  • Rotate Left: Press Q
  • Hold Shape: Press X

Gameplay Tips

To succeed in Tetris, aim to fill complete rows with blocks. Completing rows clears them away and earns you points. Try not to let the blocks reach the top of the screen!
